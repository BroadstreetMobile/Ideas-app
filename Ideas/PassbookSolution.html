Passbook Tutorial

Sample Story

Subs R Us is a local restaurant looking to attract more customers to eat at their restaurant more often. When the issue a Coupon pass to a customer, the location of some restaurants close to the customer will be added to the pass.

When the customer is next in the vicinity of the restaurant, the pass pops itself on the Lock Screen of the customer, encouraging them to come to the restaurant.

Client-side

  • MKMapKit
  • CLLocation
  • AddressBookUI

Server-side

  • PHP-PassKit
  • Slim Framework
